The Vision Behind JEEVIKSHA

For decades, India's rural workforce has been trapped in an informal system dictated by exploitative intermediaries and informational asymmetry. Our vision is to bring dignity, transparency, and digital access to rural labor. We are replacing word-of-mouth hiring with a merit-based, transaction-backed digital infrastructure that protects livelihoods and secures fair wages.

Re-Engineering Rural Employment

Comparing the traditional exploitative thekedar model against our modern digital ecosystem.

The Traditional Problem

  • Thekedar System Exploitation

    Exploitative middlemen dictate arbitrary wages and siphon off labourers' true daily earnings through steep commissions and informal debt traps.

  • Chronic Seasonal Supply Deficits

    Agricultural districts face severe, time-sensitive labour shortages during peak Kharif and Rabi cycles due to uncoordinated regional mobility.

  • Informational Asymmetry

    Employers lack real-time visibility into local labour pools, while workers lack verifiable professional identities or wage histories.

The JEEVIKSHA Solution

  • 0% Worker Commission

    Laborers face zero commission charges. They retain 100% of their hard-earned manual daily wages directly routed to their bank accounts.

  • Algorithmic Matching Engine

    Processes employer demands in real-time, cross-referencing precise geofenced pin codes to deploy labor exactly where and when required.

  • Progressive Web App (PWA)

    Built on an optimised HTML, CSS, and JS stack designed to run smoothly on budget smartphones and low-bandwidth connections.

Built for Bharat's Dual Market

Tailored technology solving two distinct challenges with one unified ecosystem.
